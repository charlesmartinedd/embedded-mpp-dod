Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Execute effective kickoff meetings within the required 30-day window
    MPP SOP
    Page 30
    "The Services/ Defense Agencies' PM shall hold an initial kickoff meeting within the first 30 days of the agreement award (Appendix L and Appendix M)."
  • Conduct Quarterly Agreement Reviews (QARs) that drive progress
    MPP SOP
    Page 30
    "Services/ Defense Agencies' PMs shall conduct quarterly assessments to determine program status and address any issues, questions, or concerns."
    MPP SOP
    Pages 22, 31-32
    For reimbursable agreements: "Agency PMs will ensure DCMA and other stakeholders are present during the agency's QARs." QARs cover scheduling, expenditures, certification status, training courses, and other items pertinent to performance.
  • Prepare and deliver Quarterly Program Management Reviews (PMRs) to DoD leadership
    MPP SOP
    Page 31
    "Quarterly Program Management Reviews (PMRs) are required to be briefed to the DoD OSBP MPP Director. Each Service/Defense Agency MPP PM shall complete a quarterly PMR slide deck and submit the slide deck to the DoD OSBP 48 hours prior to the scheduled quarterly PMR meeting."
    MPP SOP
    Page 31
    "See Quarterly PMR Review Slides Template for the quarterly PMR Review Slides. Please DO NOT alter the template format. On average, a minimum of 25% of the MPAs managed by the Service/Defense Agency MPP PM will be briefed."
